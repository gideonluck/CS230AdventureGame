The goal of this game is to check buildings on campus for your school supplies, before going to class where you'll need them. 
Some items need to be found before certain buildings can be checked.
You have to have your ID before you can look for other items in the Rec Center.
You must find all of your items before you can get the win state inside the business building. 

Some buildings let you do building specific things in them.(You can eat in the bean, try that one out.)
